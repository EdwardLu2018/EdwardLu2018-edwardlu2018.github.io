Research

My current research interests lie at the intersection of Embedded Systems and Augmented Reality. My work explores emerging technologies for mobile and IoT platforms that use real-world hardware to sense and localize devices for practical augmented, virtual, and mixed reality (combined to be called eXtended Reality, or XR) applications.
